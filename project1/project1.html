Keegan's Super Fun Blog Time

This week's blog: Aren't trucks just super cool?

Man, this week I was just inspired, for the 16th week in a row, to write about how cool trucks are. I know that some people (meanies) may say that only writing about trucks for 16 weeks is "boring" and "a waste of time and server space" but seriously, trucks are what I, a ten year old boy, live for. From their wheels to how big they are, trucks are just super cool. Not to mention how many different trucks there are. Dumpster trucks (also known by many as garbage trucks), pickup trucks, fire trucks, monster trucks, ice cream trucks, and countless other trucks, all of which are super important to the truck community. To other truck enthusiasts, please don't be upset if I left out one of your favorite trucks, or even your second or third favorite. There are so many trucks and different uses for trucks that honestly it is hard to keep track.

I hope you found this blog as stimulating and well written as I did, and I hope to see you back next week for another installment in Keegan's Super Fun Blog Time!
